Project Summary

Topic & Purpose:

My website will be a modern, responsive, info website that unites my family's three sister Vietnamese pho restaurants under one brand and one story (Pho Anh Dao, Alameda; Pho Anh Dao, Oakland; & Pho Benny, Lafayette). It will showcase each restaurant's individual menus, locations, pictures, online ordering, "about us", etc. The intended audience is food-enjoyers in the Bay Area who are looking for a place to eat Vietnamese cuisine and need more information on our story and food. The website will aim to provide visitors with an easy-to-navigate experience that reflects the authenticity of our dishes. The website uses a warm design that highlights the comfort and authenticity of Vietnamese dining, with elegant, clean, balanced layouts. Its color scheme blends earthy tones with vibrant accents.

Features:

Form

There is a “Contact Us” form on the bottom of each main page to send a message.

Transitions

The text “A Bowl For Every Story.” fades in when hovering the main image above in the homepage. It uses the transition: opacity feature.
