About Us:

Pho Collective brings together three beloved, family-owned Vietnamese restaurants that have served the Bay Area community for over a decade: Pho Anh Dao in Alameda and Oakland, and Pho Benny in Lafayette. Though each location has its own personality, we share one mission — to offer authentic, comforting Vietnamese cuisine made with tradition, passion, and fresh ingredients.

Our story begins with our family's roots in Vietnam, where recipes were passed down through generations. When we opened our first restaurant, we wanted to recreate the flavors and warmth of home. Today, Pho Collective continues that tradition by handcrafting rich broths simmered for hours, preparing fresh herbs daily, and serving every bowl of pho with the same care we would for our own family.

As our community has grown, so has our commitment to creating a welcoming dining experience. Whether you're visiting us for a quick lunch, a family gathering, or a warm bowl of pho on a chilly day, we're proud to share our table with you.

Explore our menus, browse photos, order online, or learn about catering for your next event — all through our modern, responsive website. Thank you for supporting family-owned restaurants and being a part of the Pho Collective family.
