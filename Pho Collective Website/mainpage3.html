Welcome to Pho Anh Dao!

At Phở Anh Đào in Alameda, we serve traditional phở, fresh spring rolls, bánh mì, and a wide variety of authentic Vietnamese dishes. Every meal is crafted with fresh, quality ingredients to bring out the true flavors of Vietnam. Phở Anh Đào is built on a deep passion for Vietnamese cooking and a dedication to sharing comforting, time-honored recipes with the community. Since opening our Alameda location, our team has taken pride in preparing delicious food and offering warm, welcoming service to every guest who walks through our doors.

Authentic Pho Dac Biet

A warm bowl of Phở Đặc Biệt greets you with a rich, clear broth layered with the aromas of slow-simmered beef bones, ginger, and star anise. Soft rice noodles cradle a generous assortment of tender brisket, rare steak, beef meatballs, and tripe, each adding its own depth of flavor. Fresh cilantro, scallions, and thin onion petals rest on top, ready to mingle with a squeeze of lime and a handful of crisp bean sprouts. The result is a hearty, fragrant, and deeply satisfying bowl that offers a perfect blend of savory richness and refreshing brightness in every sip.

Restaurant

The restaurant's interior feels warm and inviting, with gentle lighting that sets a relaxed, welcoming mood. At the center, a full bar offers a rotating selection of beers, wines, and refreshing cocktails—perfect for an easy, unhurried drink. Soft music fills the space while TVs glow quietly in the background, adding a comfortable, casual energy. On one side, guests can enjoy a classic phở house atmosphere with cozy tables for both groups and solo diners. On the other, a dedicated crawfish section brings lively flavor and excitement, creating a unique dual-concept dining experience. Whether you're craving a steaming bowl of phở or a seafood feast, the space brings both worlds together in one vibrant, flavorful setting.
