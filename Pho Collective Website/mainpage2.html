OAKLAND

Welcome to Pho Anh Dao!

At Phở Anh Đào - Oakland, we proudly serve traditional phở, fresh spring rolls, flavorful bánh mì, and a variety of authentic Vietnamese dishes. Every bowl and plate is made with care, using fresh, high-quality ingredients to deliver the vibrant flavors of Vietnam. Owned by the dedicated culinary team behind Anh Đào, the restaurant reflects a lifelong passion for cooking and sharing comforting, time-honored recipes. Since opening in Oakland, Phở Anh Đào has remained committed to serving the community with warm hospitality, delicious food, and the authentic taste of Vietnamese cuisine.

Authentic Pork and Seafood Noodle Soup

A warm bowl of pork and seafood noodle soup greets you with a clear, savory broth enriched by simmered pork bones and the subtle sweetness of the sea. Soft rice noodles cradle tender slices of pork alongside succulent shrimp and delicate pieces of squid. Fresh cilantro, scallions, and thin onion petals add brightness, while a squeeze of lime and a handful of crisp bean sprouts lift the flavors, creating a comforting blend of rich, fragrant, and refreshing notes in every sip.

Restaurant

The restaurant’s interior feels spacious and inviting, with warm lighting that creates a relaxed, comfortable mood. Lush green plants are placed throughout the large dining area, bringing a fresh, natural touch to the room. A gently bubbling fish tank adds a calming focal point, while TVs mounted around the space provide a soft, casual background energy. Generous seating options accommodate both groups and solo diners, making it easy to settle in and enjoy your meal in this open, tranquil setting—without the need for a patio or bar.
