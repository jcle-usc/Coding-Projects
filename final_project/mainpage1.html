Pho Benny

Welcome to Pho Benny!

At Pho Benny we serve traditional Pho, spring rolls, Banh Mi and so much more. We use fresh ingredients for every dish. Benny Tran, the owner of Pho Benny has always had a passion for cooking. Opening officially on April 19, 2021, Benny was able to finally pursue his dream of being able to start a restaurant; cooking and serving Vietnamese food with his team to the public.

Authentic Pho Ga

A warm bowl of Phở Gà greets you with a clear golden broth scented with ginger and star anise. Soft rice noodles nestle beneath tender slices of chicken, topped with fresh cilantro, scallions, and thin onion petals. A squeeze of lime and a few crisp bean sprouts brighten the bowl, creating a comforting blend of fragrant, savory, and refreshing flavors in every sip.

Restaurant

The restaurant's interior feels warm and relaxed, with dim lighting that sets a cozy mood. A sit-down bar anchors the space, stocked with a rotating selection of beers and wines for an easy, unhurried drink. Soft music fills the room while TVs glow quietly in the background, adding a casual energy. Inside, tables offer comfortable seating for groups or solo diners, and a small patio extends the atmosphere outdoors, perfect for catching a breeze while enjoying your bowl of phở.
